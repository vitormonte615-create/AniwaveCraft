# Aniwave Craft
Aniwave Craft is an ambitious open-world survival RPG built in Unity, designed for immersive exploration, deep progression, and limitless creativity. Set in a vast, ever-changing sky realm, players awaken in a world of floating islands where survival depends on ingenuity, courage, and mastery of both craft and combat.

Gather resources, build your own flying base, and shape your journey across a vertical world filled with unique biomes, hidden ruins, and dangerous dungeons. From peaceful low-altitude landscapes to hostile high-altitude territories, every layer of the world introduces new challenges, enemies, and rare materials to discover.

Forge your path with a flexible RPG system that allows you to mix combat styles, magic abilities, and engineering skills without being locked into a single class. Engage in fast-paced battles using melee weapons, ranged tools, or arcane powers, while upgrading your gear through a deep crafting and progression system.

Build and customize your airborne home piece by piece—your base is not just shelter, but your key to exploration. Expand, upgrade, and pilot it to reach new horizons, uncover secrets, and survive increasingly dangerous environments.

Whether playing solo or with friends, Aniwave Craft delivers a dynamic sandbox experience where exploration, construction, and combat are seamlessly connected. Every decision shapes your adventure, and every journey into the unknown brings new opportunities—and new risks.

Key Features:

Expansive open-world sky environment with layered vertical exploration
Deep crafting, building, and survival mechanics
Flexible RPG progression with skills, magic, and equipment
Dynamic combat with multiple playstyles
Fully customizable flying base system
Solo and co-op gameplay support
Procedurally varied islands, dungeons, and biomes

Aniwave Craft invites you to rise above the clouds and create your own story in a world where the sky is not the limit—it’s just the beginning.
